## Church Administrator (Part-Time) – St John’s Park, Sheffield

St John’s Park is seeking a warm, organised and motivated **Church Administrator** to support the life and mission of our church.

### About the role

This key role provides office administration, manages bookings and enquiries, supports Church of England statutory processes (weddings, baptisms and funerals), and acts as a welcoming first point of contact for our community.

### Key details

- **20 hours per week**
- **£25,500 pro rata**
- **Permanent (funded until 31 Dec 2030)**

We are looking for someone with strong organisational and IT skills, excellent communication, and a heart to see the church flourish.

**Closing date: Monday 2nd February, 9:00am**
